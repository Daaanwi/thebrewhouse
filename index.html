Welcome to The Brew House

Order Now

We are The Brew House.

At the Brew House, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage - it's a ritual. That's why we source only the finest beans from around the world and roast them in-house to perfection. Our skilled baristas then craft each cup with care, so that every sip is a moment of pure indulgence.

As a family-owned business, we take pride in providing our customers with not only the best coffee and tea, but also a warm and welcoming atmosphere. From our cozy seating areas to our friendly staff, we strive to make the Brew House feel like a home away from home. So come on in, grab a cup of your favorite brew, and stay awhile.

Our Menu

Espresso

A single shot of our signature espresso, made with beans sourced from local farmers. Rich and robust with a smooth finish. Available in small, medium, and large.

Latte

Made with espresso and steamed milk, this classic drink is perfect for those who want a little extra creaminess in their coffee. Available in small, medium, and large.

Cold Brew

Our cold brew coffee is steeped for 12 hours, resulting in a smooth and refreshing drink. Served over ice with a splash of milk. Available in small and large.

Cappuccino

This classic Italian coffee is made with a shot of espresso, steamed milk, and a layer of frothed milk on top. The cappuccino has a strong, bold flavor with a creamy texture.

Mocha

A decadent coffee drink made with a shot of espresso, steamed milk, and a rich chocolate syrup. Our mochas are topped with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder.

Americano

A classic coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and hot water. The Americano has a bold, rich flavor that's similar to drip coffee, but with a smoother, more nuanced taste.
