About Us

We are The Brew House.

At The Brew House, we're passionate about coffee. Our shop was founded by two friends who wanted to share their love of quality coffee with their community. We believe that every cup of coffee should be made with care and attention to detail, which is why we use only the finest beans and the best brewing techniques. Stop by and say hello - we can't wait to share our love of coffee with you.

At The Brew House, we're committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing. We believe that it's important to support coffee farmers and ensure that they are paid fairly for their hard work. That's why we source our coffee beans directly from farmers who use sustainable farming practices and pay them a fair price. We also strive to reduce waste by using eco-friendly packaging and encouraging our customers to bring their own reusable cups. By choosing to support The Brew House, you're not just getting a great cup of coffee - you're also supporting a sustainable, ethical business.

Our Menu

Espresso

A single shot of our signature espresso, made with beans sourced from local farmers. Rich and robust with a smooth finish. Available in small, medium, and large.

Latte

Made with espresso and steamed milk, this classic drink is perfect for those who want a little extra creaminess in their coffee. Available in small, medium, and large.

Cold Brew

Our cold brew coffee is steeped for 12 hours, resulting in a smooth and refreshing drink. Served over ice with a splash of milk. Available in small and large.

Cappuccino

This classic Italian coffee is made with a shot of espresso, steamed milk, and a layer of frothed milk on top. The cappuccino has a strong, bold flavor with a creamy texture.

Mocha

A decadent coffee drink made with a shot of espresso, steamed milk, and a rich chocolate syrup. Our mochas are topped with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder, making it the perfect indulgence on a chilly day.

Americano

A classic coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and hot water. The Americano has a bold, rich flavor that's similar to drip coffee, but with a smoother, more nuanced taste. It's a great choice for those who enjoy a strong cup of coffee without the added milk or sweetness.
